Africa's Talking Open Hackathon: FinTech

Join us at our open hackathon every last Thursday of the month. Come build with us, present feature request, give feedback or just contribute to our open source API's and SDKs. Get your ticket, let us exchange knowledge, best practices, submit feature requests and innovate.

THU,Jun 30, 9:00 AM (EAT)

100 RSVP'ed

Agenda

Warm-up

Introduction and Pitching, Form teams where need be. Building/hacking, Feedback, Feature requests.

Code

Code in GitHub/GitLab/BitBucket and deploy in Docker/Automated CI/Travis CI/Bamboo

Demo & Wrap-up

Teams or individuals will demo what they have hacked for feedback or adoption.
